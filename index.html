About Us

Founded by John Kornos-recognized chef and world renowned chef Abigail Henderson, Guama has been a sunny staple for scratch-made eats and sweets in St. Paul, MN since opening our doors in 1980. In fall of 2021 we expanded our love of community and good food to open a second location in NE Minneapolis! Just like our building in Lowertown, our Minneapolis corner location is surrounded by glass, sunny light, and friendly faces.
